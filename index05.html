About

Welcome! My name is Sarah Griebel. I'm a PhD student in Information Sciences at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I'm interested in the intersection of natural language processing and computational social sciences, particularly in the realm of literary machine translation.

I received my MFA from the University of Iowa's Translation Workshop. At Illinois, I'm advised by Professor Ted Underwood and Professor J. Stephen Downie.
